# HAIN Master Navigation

## HAIN at a glance

HAIN stands for **Hybrid AI Native**.

It is a framework for thinking about modern intelligent systems across **cloud**, **edge**, and **agentic** contexts through clearer public structure, cleaner architectural language, and structured narrative assets.

At its current stage, HAIN is not presented as a finished commercial platform. It is presented as a structured framework system with a public-facing identity, an architecture layer, a practical direction, and a growing body of narrative and terminology support.

---

## Core system map

The current HAIN system is organized around three core layers:

### HAIN World
The public-facing identity and umbrella entry point.

Its role is to introduce the framework through public websites, repositories, and outward-facing materials.

### HAINOA
The open architecture layer.

Its role is to explain the structure, principles, and interpretive logic of the framework.

### HAINIZE
The toolkit and developer-oriented direction.

Its role is to point toward practical entry, reusable assets, templates, and future implementation-facing materials.

In simplified form:

- **HAIN World** introduces
- **HAINOA** explains
- **HAINIZE** enables practical entry

# Public Entry Points

## Overview

The HAIN framework is currently expressed through a small but deliberate set of public-facing entry points.

These entry points do not all serve the same purpose. Some function as brand surfaces, some as architecture layers, and some as documentation or public reference channels. Together, they form the first visible shell of the HAIN system.

This structure is useful because it separates identity, architecture, repositories, and external visibility into distinct but connected surfaces.

## Main site

**https://hainworld.com**

The main site is the primary [public entry point](https://github.com/hainworldhq/hain-glossary/blob/main/terms/public-entry-point.md) of the framework.

Its role is to introduce HAIN World as the [umbrella identity](https://github.com/hainworldhq/hain-glossary/blob/main/terms/umbrella-identity.md) for the Hybrid AI Native concept. It is the most natural place for first contact, top-level explanation, and outward-facing presentation.

The main site should remain the clearest and most stable public surface in the system.

## Short link

**https://hain.world**

The short link exists as a branded redirect and lightweight access point.

Its role is not to replace the main site, but to support cleaner sharing, easier recall, and stronger brand continuity around the HAIN World name.

In practice, it functions as a compact public alias for the broader framework identity.

## Architecture site

**https://hainoa.org**

The architecture site is the theory- and structure-oriented entry point.

Its role is to present HAINOA as the [open architecture layer](https://github.com/hainworldhq/hain-glossary/blob/main/terms/open-architecture-layer.md) within the HAIN framework. Compared with the main site, it is more focused on principles, conceptual structure, and architectural interpretation.

If HAIN World introduces the framework, HAINOA explains its structural logic.

## GitHub

**https://github.com/hainworldhq**

GitHub is the repository and asset management entry point.

It functions as the place where the public framework structure, websites, glossary, whitepaper materials, and supporting repositories are organized. It also serves as an important signal that HAIN is not only a naming concept, but a structured documentation system with versioned public assets.

At the current stage, GitHub acts more as a knowledge and architecture hub than as a software delivery platform.

## LinkedIn

**https://linkedin.com/company/hainworld**

LinkedIn is the public narrative and professional visibility entry point.

Its role is to support outward-facing communication, positioning, and lightweight public updates around HAIN World. Compared with the websites and repositories, LinkedIn is less about structure and more about presence.

It provides a social-facing layer for the framework without requiring the creation of multiple fragmented brand accounts.

## Email

**hello@hainworld.com**

The email entry point provides a direct and formal contact channel for the framework.

Its role is simple but important: it makes the public-facing shell feel more complete, more reachable, and more brand-consistent. Even at an early stage, a stable branded email address improves coherence across the websites, repositories, and public company page.

## Why these entry points matter

A framework becomes easier to understand when its public surfaces are not collapsed into one place.

Different audiences arrive through different paths. Some discover a concept through a website. Some want architectural explanation. Some look for repository structure. Some want a professional page or a direct contact channel.

By keeping these entry points distinct but coordinated, the HAIN framework becomes easier to navigate and easier to evolve.

This is especially important at an early stage, when structure matters more than scale.

## Current mapping

The current public-facing mapping can be summarized as follows:

- **HAIN World** — main public identity
- **hainworld.com** — primary website
- **hain.world** — short branded redirect
- **hainoa.org** — architecture and theory layer
- **github.com/hainworldhq** — repository and asset hub
- **linkedin.com/company/hainworld** — public positioning channel
- **hello@hainworld.com** — direct contact channel

## Working summary

The public entry points of HAIN are intentionally distributed across brand, architecture, repository, social, and contact layers.

This allows the framework to remain clear, navigable, and coherent while still being flexible enough to grow over time.
